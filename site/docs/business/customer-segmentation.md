# Customer Segmentation

---

## Segment 1 · Power Developer (Individual)

**Profile:** Senior engineer, 5+ years experience, ships features daily, already using AI coding tools, frustrated by wrong plans.

**Trigger:** Has experienced at least one AI-caused regression or spent significant time debugging a plan that looked correct but was structurally wrong.

**Value prop:** Nirnex makes AI tools more reliable without changing the workflow. Install in 5 minutes, Lane A runs silently forever.

**Conversion path:** Free tier → personal license on first Lane C task.

---

## Segment 2 · Tech Lead / Engineering Manager

**Profile:** Owns quality outcomes for a 3–10 person team. Uses AI tools team-wide but is anxious about AI-caused regressions in production.

**Trigger:** Team had a production incident where AI-assisted code introduced a regression that tests didn't catch.

**Value prop:** The Decision Ledger gives the lead visibility into every AI-assisted decision made on the codebase. Ground truth reviews make improvement systematic.

**Conversion path:** Team trial → team license after first calibration cycle.

---

## Segment 3 · Platform / DevEx Team

**Profile:** Builds internal tooling for a 50+ person engineering org. Evaluating AI coding tools at scale.

**Trigger:** Needs to provide AI coding tools to the org with governance, auditability, and quality guarantees.

**Value prop:** Nirnex provides the evidence and audit layer that makes AI coding tools enterprise-safe. Operational contracts, immutable traces, human gates.

**Conversion path:** Pilot with 5 teams → enterprise agreement.
